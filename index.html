Sand Cat

Felis Margarita.

The sand cat, also known as the sand dune cat, is native to North Africa, the Middle East, and Central Asia. It is the only true cat that lives primarily in desert regions, and with it's thickly-furred feet, it is well-suited to a harsh climate with both extreme heat and cold.

    Sand Cat Scientific Classifications:

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Suborder: Feliformia
  • Family: Felidae
  • Subfamily: Felinae
  • Genus: Felis
  • Species: F. margarita
